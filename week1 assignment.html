PROJECT SECTION

Warranty Claim Fraud Detection DataAnalysis

In this research, we showcase the application of analytics in aiding manufacturers to identify fraudulent activities within their warranty claims information. Conventional approaches to processing claims involve manual assessment by an auditor adhering to predefined rules. Through the utilization of analytics, this procedure is automated, allowing technology to uncover intricate data patterns imperceptible to an individual. Examples of fraud instances where analytics proves valuable encompass:

  • Fabricated clients and/or claims
  • Duplicate charging for replaced components
  • Superfluous or unwarranted repairs/part replacements

It is important to note that while analytics is not designed to supplant the existing claims processing system, it should serve as a secondary review mechanism to uncover atypical patterns and suspicious connections indicative of potential fraud
